Workflow Process – S.I.O™

Structure

Services

Fund Launch

  • Initial Setup: Work with the investment advisor and legal/tax counsel to structure the investment fund.
  • Vendor Selection: Leverage strategic industry relationships to select a custom suite of fund service providers.
  • Implementation and Integration: Execute on initial setup, vendor selection and optimize the process flow.

Operational Due Diligence (ODD), Fundraising and Marketing

  • Participate in fundraising and investor due diligence meetings.
  • Speak at conferences as the CFO of the Investment Advisor.
  • Coordinate investor-led Operational Due Diligence reviews.

Compliance

Establish an institutional quality compliance program and produce materials to comply with U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) regulations.

Core Fund

  • Accounting and Financial Reporting: Oversee and review administrator prepared books and records including fee calculations, net asset value and internal rate of return.
  • Audit & Tax: Work with fund auditors and tax accountants to set up, plan and issue the audited financial statements (GAAP and IFRS standards) and regulatory tax filings.

Core Management Company

  • Accounting: Monthly bookkeeping including accounts payable and receivable, wire preparation, budgeting and cashflow forecasting.
  • Financial Statement Preparation: Quarterly preparation and review of management company financial statements.
  • Regulatory & Tax: Work with tax advisor to file quarterly tax estimates and year-end tax returns.
